Expertise: Heat Transfer; Fluid Mechanics; Porous Media »

Dr. Kiran Kumar K is an Post-Doctoral Fellow in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati under the supervision of Prof. Niranjan Sahoo. He graduated (B.E) in Mechanical Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru National college of Engineering in 2012 and M.Tech (2017) in Thermal Power Engineering and Ph.D. (2024) in Mechanical Engineering Scinces, from University B.D.T College of Engineering Davangere, under Visvesvaraya Technological University Belagavi, Karnataka, India. He has 10 years of Teaching experience and at last served as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Enigeerring at PES Institute of Technology & Management (2017 - 2024). He is been recognized as an NPTEL Translator (from Aug-2020 to till date) and published 10 Books as one of the translator in Kannada Language (NPTEL Online Courses) funded by Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, and also serves as an Reviewer for the International Journals.

His primary research interests include Flow and heat transfer through porous media, particularly in metal foams. He has published 12 international journal papers, 2 book chapters, and presented papers in International conference (5) and National conference (3).
